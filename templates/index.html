Our Data

New York Times Best Sellers
Our Data is from two lists, and includes Best Sellers from January 2020 to October 2020

The word cloud to the left is cycling through descriptions of each book our database.

Although most of our data is from the New York Times Books API, we also pulled in ratings data from the GoodReads API

Our goal was to examine some of the best selling books of this year, especially as the COVID-19 pandemic increased the popularity of reading.
